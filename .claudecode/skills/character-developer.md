# Character Development Assistant

## Purpose
Creates detailed character profiles with backgrounds, motivations, and development arcs.

## Usage
Use when developing new characters or deepening existing ones for your book.

## Instructions
For each character, generate:
1. Basic Information (name, age, appearance)
2. Background and History
3. Personality Traits (5-7 key traits)
4. Motivations and Goals
5. Fears and Weaknesses
6. Relationships with other characters
7. Character arc throughout the story

## Template
```
Character Name: [Name]
Age: [Age]
Role: [Protagonist/Antagonist/Supporting]

Background:
- Family history
- Important life events
- Education and skills

Personality:
- Trait 1: Brave - Never backs down from challenges
- Trait 2: Impulsive - Acts before thinking
- Trait 3: Loyal - Protects friends at any cost

Goals:
- External: What they want to achieve
- Internal: What they need to learn

Weaknesses:
- Trust issues from past betrayal
- Fear of failure

Character Arc:
- Beginning: Selfish and isolated
- Middle: Learns to trust others
- End: Becomes a team leader
```

## Best Practices
- Make characters three-dimensional with flaws
- Include contradictions (brave but insecure)
- Ensure actions align with personality
- Show growth and change over time

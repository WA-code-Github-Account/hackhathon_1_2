# Research Skill

## Purpose
Gathers accurate information, facts, and reference material for book writing to ensure authenticity and depth.

## Usage
Use this skill when you need to research topics, verify facts, or gather background information for your book.

## Instructions
When conducting research:
1. Identify the research topic or query
2. Search for reliable and credible sources
3. Gather relevant facts, statistics, and information
4. Verify information accuracy across multiple sources
5. Organize findings in a structured format
6. Cite sources properly

## Research Categories
- **Historical Research**: Dates, events, timelines, historical accuracy
- **Technical Research**: Scientific concepts, technologies, terminologies
- **Cultural Research**: Customs, traditions, social norms, languages
- **Geographic Research**: Locations, climates, terrain, settings
- **Character Research**: Professions, skills, backgrounds, motivations

## Output Format
```
RESEARCH SUMMARY
Topic: [Research topic]
Date: [Current date]

KEY FINDINGS:
1. [Finding with source]
2. [Finding with source]
3. [Finding with source]

DETAILED INFORMATION:
- [Category 1]: [Detailed info]
- [Category 2]: [Detailed info]

SOURCES:
1. [Source 1 - URL/Book/Reference]
2. [Source 2]
3. [Source 3]

RECOMMENDED FOR:
- Character backgrounds
- Setting descriptions
- Plot authenticity
```

## Best Practices
- Always verify information from multiple sources
- Use authoritative and credible sources (academic, official, expert)
- Keep research organized by topic/category
- Note publication dates for time-sensitive information
- Save sources for future reference and citations
- Cross-reference facts to ensure accuracy

## Example Use Cases
- Research 1950s New York for period novel setting
- Gather information about AI technology for sci-fi book
- Research medical procedures for thriller with doctor protagonist
- Study medieval warfare for fantasy battle scenes
- Investigate criminal investigation procedures for mystery novel
